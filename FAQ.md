# Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

### What is EduPro Academy?
EduPro Academy is an online learning platform offering courses in web development, data science, design, marketing, and more. We provide high-quality education to learners worldwide.

### How do I enroll in a course?
1. Browse our course catalog
2. Click "Enroll Now" on your chosen course
3. Create an account or sign in
4. Complete the payment process
5. Start learning immediately!

### What payment methods do you accept?
- Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
- PayPal
- Bank Transfer (for bulk enrollments)

### Can I get a refund?
Yes! We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied, contact our support team within 30 days for a full refund.

## Courses

### How long do I have access to a course?
Once enrolled, you have **lifetime access** to the course materials, including all future updates.

### Do I get a certificate?
Yes! Upon completing all course requirements and passing the final assessment, you'll receive a certificate of completion that you can:
- Download as PDF
- Share on LinkedIn
- Print for your portfolio

### Are the courses self-paced?
Absolutely! All our courses are self-paced. Learn at your own speed, anytime, anywhere.

### Can I download course materials?
Yes! Most course materials are downloadable, including:
- Video lessons (with premium app)
- PDF guides
- Source code
- Exercise files

### What if I get stuck?
We provide multiple support channels:
- Course Q&A section
- Community forums
- Live chat support
- Instructor email support

- iOS (App Store)

### Do you offer offline learning?
Yes! With our mobile app, you can download courses and learn without an internet connection.

## Account

### How do I reset my password?
1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for reset instructions

### Can I have multiple accounts?
We recommend one account per person. Having multiple accounts may result in loss of progress.

### How do I delete my account?
Contact our support team at support@edupro.academy. Note: This action is irreversible.
